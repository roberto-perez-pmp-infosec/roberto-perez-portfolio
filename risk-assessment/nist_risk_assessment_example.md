# Cybersecurity Risk Assessment (NIST Framework)

## Overview

This project demonstrates a structured cybersecurity risk assessment using principles from the NIST Cybersecurity Framework.

The objective was to identify risks to organizational assets and recommend mitigation strategies.

## Scope

Assessment focused on:

- Network infrastructure
- Cloud services
- User access controls
- Data storage practices

## Risk Identification

Key risks identified included:

- Weak access control policies
- Misconfigured cloud storage permissions
- Lack of centralized logging
- Inconsistent patch management

## Risk Analysis

Risks were evaluated based on:

- Likelihood of exploitation
- Potential business impact
- Existing security controls

## Risk Mitigation Strategies

Recommended mitigation actions included:

- Implementing role-based access control (RBAC)
- Enforcing multi-factor authentication
- Improving logging and monitoring
- Establishing patch management policies

## Outcome

The assessment provided a roadmap to strengthen the organization's security posture and reduce exposure to common cyber threats.

## Lessons Learned

Effective risk management requires continuous monitoring, governance, and alignment between technical teams and business leadership.
